The collection follows a three-tier classification system:

  • Foundation Domains: Category cornerstones (e.g., BEAUTYXX.COM, FASHIONXX.COM, FOODXX.COM, TECHXX.COM)
  • Primary Domains: Major category elements (e.g., JEWELRYXX.COM, CHOCOLATEXX.COM, TOYXX.COM)
  • Secondary Domains: Specialized niches (e.g., BROOCHXX.COM, CALENDARXX.COM, CUFFLINKSXX.COM)

In 43 out of 45 carefully researched categories, GENEXXO has assembled over 90% coverage of essential terms. The collection's architecture recognizes both finite categories (well-defined fields where GENEXXO has secured virtually every meaningful term) and expansive categories (broader fields with unlimited potential niches where GENEXXO has secured all prominent elements).
